About

Initially, I began my career in the automotive industry, specializing in advanced German automobiles. I worked extensively with high-performance vehicles, applying my technical expertise to diagnose and resolve complex issues. While I enjoyed the dynamic nature of the work and the challenge of working with cutting-edge technology, I began to feel that I was not fully leveraging my potential.

This realization prompted a shift in my career focus. I transitioned into software development, where I found that my strong analytical skills, attention to detail, and problem-solving abilities were directly transferable. Today, I am excited to be working in software design, where I can apply my creativity to develop innovative solutions that make a meaningful impact.

Beyond my professional career, I am passionate about hiking. During the summer months, I spent much of my free time hiking in the mountains, an experience that not only helped me recharge but also reinforced values such as resilience and perseverance—qualities I bring to both my personal and professional life.
